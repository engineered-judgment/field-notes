# AI Use Policy

**Audience:** Students and faculty

Use AI to generate alternatives, not to replace accountability.

## Required declaration format
Include this block in every AI-assisted entry:

```text
AI-assisted step:
Tool/model used:
What I accepted:
What I rejected:
Independent check performed:
Effect on final decision:
```

## Minimum policy standard
1. Declare what AI produced and where it was used.
2. Verify key claims independently.
3. Keep final judgment human and decision ownership explicit.

## Compliant vs non-compliant
**Compliant:**
- "AI drafted three alternatives; I rejected two after unit and bounds checks."
- "I verified the accepted claim using an independent back-of-envelope estimate."
- "Final recommendation changed after verification showed one AI claim was invalid."

**Non-compliant:**
- "AI generated the answer and I submitted it."
- "No verification method documented."
- "No record of what evidence changed the decision."
